## THE THREE BATTLES TO WIN

### Battle 1: Discovery (Immediate — the current pain point)
**Problem:** 200K-500K+ skills, no way to find the right one.
**Solution:** Semantic search + capability trees + trust-weighted ranking + LLM enrichment.
**Reference:** SkillsGate (semantic), AgentSkillOS (capability trees), SkillFlow (trust metrics).

### Battle 2: Trust (Near-term — the enterprise unlock)
**Problem:** 26.1% vulnerability rate, CVE-2026-25253, infostealer attacks.
**Solution:** Formal verification (SkillFortify pattern) + 4-tier governance + locked identities + transaction-based reviews.
**Reference:** SkillFortify (96.95% F1), Agensi.io (8-point scan), GenAgentTrust Hub.

### Battle 3: Commerce (Medium-term — the business model)
**Problem:** Skills are valuable but no payment infrastructure exists.
**Solution:** Three-tier pricing (Rent/Learn/Own) + Stripe Connect + creator revenue share + usage-based billing.
**Reference:** SquidBay (Lightning), LarryBrain ($3K MRR), Agent 37 (web-accessible).
